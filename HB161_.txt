STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO HUMAN SERVICES CONTRACTS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  No later than twenty days prior to the convening of the regular session of 2012, the department of human services shall submit a report to the legislature concerning contracts awarded during the period from January 1, 2007, to June 30, 2011, to nonprofit organizations that provide domestic violence services for victims of domestic violence as the organization's major service.  The report shall include the following:
     (1)  A description of the process used to award contracts to nonprofit organizations that received funds from the department of human services for domestic violence services;
     (2)  A list of nonprofit organizations that were awarded these contracts and the amount of funds awarded to each nonprofit organization receiving a contract; and
     (3)  The number of victims that received domestic violence services and the types of assistance they received under these contracts.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Domestic Violence; DHS Report on Nonprofit Service Providers
 
Description:
Requires department of human services to report to legislature prior to 2012 regular session information relating to domestic violence victim services contract award process; awardees, dollar amounts, number of victims served, and types of services provided by nonprofit organizations for the period from 1/1/2007 to 6/30/2011.
