STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITOL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR KAHULUI HARBOR.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the harbor special fund created under section 266-19, Hawaii Revised Statutes, for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     1.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Kahului Harbor, Maui
          Plans, design, and construction of a protruding pier structure dedicated to fuel transfer.
          Plans                             $     1,000
          Design                            $   399,000
          Construction                      $48,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.

Report Title:
Kahului Harbor; Capital Improvement Project; Fuel Pier
