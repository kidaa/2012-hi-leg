HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2791
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FORTY-FOURTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2012-2013 to be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Nanaikapono elementary school, Oahu
          Landscape, re-seed, and install sprinkler system on upper elementary field.
          Total funding                           $100,000
     B.   Nanaikapono elementary school, Oahu
          Rubberized mats for upper elementary playground equipment.
          Total funding                           $100,000
     C.   Nanaikapono elementary school, Oahu
          Exterior painting of buildings.
          Total funding                           $100,000      D.   Nanaikapono elementary school, Oahu
          Replace cement benches around campus.
          Total funding                           $100,000
     E.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          Merge the SP and T Building to create additional classrooms and media production area.
          Total funding                         $3,000,000
     F.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          Air conditioning for school rooms, cafeteria, and gym.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     G.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          New auditorium.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     H.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          Replace field scoreboard.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     I.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          Renovate gym restroom and lockers.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     J.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          Public address system for gym.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     K.   Waianae high school, Oahu
          New aluminum bleachers.
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     L.   Ka Waihona o ka Naauao public charter school, Oahu
          Plans, design, and construction for a two-story classroom building.
          Plans                                    $50,000
          Design                                   $50,000
          Construction                           7,900,000
          Total funding                         $8,000,000
     M.   Leeward community college, Oahu
          Plans, design, and construction for Waianae Education Center, Phase II.
          Plans                                    $50,000
          Design                                   $50,000
          Construction                           4,900,000
          Total funding                         $5,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
 
INTRODUCED BY:
