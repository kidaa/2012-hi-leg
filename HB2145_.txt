HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2145
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The purpose of this Act is to address economic development by establishing, as state policy, an intent to complete or facilitate the completion of a list of ten specific projects within the next ten-year period.
     SECTION 2.  Chapter 226, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new part to be appropriately designated and to read as follows:
"PART   .  PROJECTS TO BE COMPLETED BY DECEMBER 31, 2023
     §226-     Ten projects to be completed by December 31, 2023.  It is the policy of the State to complete or facilitate the completion of the following projects by December 31, 2023:
     (1)  Broadband infrastructure that provides coverage for one hundred per cent of the following land areas in the State: land zoned for business, commercial, industrial, hotel, or resort use and land used for higher education instruction or research;
     (2)  The airport modernization project of the department of transportation, as planned as of the effective date of this section;
     (3)  The harbor modernization project of the department of transportation, as planned as of the effective date of this section;
     (4)  The thirty-meter telescope on Mauna Kea;
     (5)  A regional bio-hazard laboratory under the University of Hawaii;
     (6)  The Diamond Head culinary institute on the slope of Diamond Head;
     (7)  The 690 Pohukaina street mixed use transit oriented development project;
     (8)  The locally preferred alternative mass transit system of the city and county of Honolulu;
     (9)  Renewable energy production facilities that are sufficient to meet the goals of the first decade of the Hawaii clean energy initiative; and
    (10)  A community college campus in West Hawaii."
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
