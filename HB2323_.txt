TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWELFTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $16,314,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary, and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2012-2013 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the twelfth representative district, as follows:
I.   To be expended by the department of health:
1.   Kula hospital, Maui
     A.   Repairs to facility         $2,500,000
              Total funding                         $2,500,000
     B.   Exterior renovations including hurricane windows upgrade, cottages   $2,900,000
              Total funding                         $2,900,000
     C.   Facility-wide ward room and building repairs and renovations     $6,720,000
              Total funding                         $6,720,000
     D.   Air conditioning system replacement and upgrades                      $396,000
               Total funding                           $396,000
     E.   Design and construction of
          Kula hospital storage facility $150,000
               Total funding                           $150,000
     F.   Repairs and renovations for
          Hale Makamae                $2,148,000
               Total funding                         $2,148,000
     G.   Master plan design
          Plans                         $500,000
              Total funding                           $500,000
II.  To be expended by the department of transportation:
          Haleakala highway and Makawao avenue, design and construction for left turn stacking lane; provided that funds are matched by the county of Maui
          Construction                $1,000,000
               Total funding                         $1,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriations that are unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 3.  The sum appropriated for the respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
