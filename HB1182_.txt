A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWENTY-FOURTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Noelani elementary school, Oahu
          Construction of multi-stalled student
          bathroom, first floor B-building.          $  300,000
              Total funding                         $  300,000
          Construction of extension of school
          Library for library resources and
          technology/media center.                   $  300,000
              Total funding                         $  300,000
     B.   Roosevelt high school, Oahu
          Master plan.                               $  250,000
              Total funding                         $  250,000
          Renovation/expansion of the gym.           $5,300,000
              Total funding                         $5,300,000
          Renovation/expansion of the bandroom.      $4,300,000
              Total funding                         $4,300,000
          Construction of a multi-level parking
          structure.                                 $4,900,000
              Total funding                         $4,900,000
     C.   Stevenson middle school, Oahu
          Plan and design for an initial master
          plan.                                      $  550,000
              Total funding                         $  550,000
          Plans, design, and construction of a
          multi-purpose science learning
          center, Building A.                        $7,200,000
              Total funding                         $7,200,000
          Plans, design, and construction of a
          multi-purpose recreational facility.       $1,500,000
              Total funding                         $1,500,000
     D.   Manoa elementary school, Oahu
          Upgrade electrical system.
              Plan and design                       $   50,000
              Construction                          $  350,000
              Total funding                         $  400,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
