STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO VOLUNTEER MEDICAL ASSISTANCE SERVICES.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Section 321-2.5, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by amending subsection (a) to read as follows:
            "(a)  This section shall apply to all volunteer medical assistance personnel, including:
     (1)  Physicians;
     (2)  Psychologists;
     (3)  Nurses;
     (4)  Emergency medical technicians;
     (5)  Social workers;
     (6)  Mobile intensive care technicians; [and]
     (7)  Physician assistants[,]; and
     (8)  Pharmacists,
licensed or certified in this State, or employed by a health care facility, while providing volunteer medical assistance services."
     SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
