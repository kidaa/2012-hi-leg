HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2710
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CONSTRUCTION OF LAWS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Section 1-18, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is repealed.
     ["§1-18  "Or", "and".  Each of the terms "or" and "and", has the meaning of the other or of both."]
     SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
 
INTRODUCED BY:
_____________________________
 
 
 

 
Report Title:
Construction of Laws; Meaning of "Or" and "And"
 
Description:
Repeals law providing that the terms "or" and "and" have the meaning of the other or of both.
