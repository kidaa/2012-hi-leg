HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2351
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS TO THE JUDICIARY.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $         or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2012-2013, for a grant-in-aid to the judiciary for the hiring of two judges and necessary staff in the family court of the first judicial circuit, to conduct jury trials for misdemeanor domestic violence and domestic violence-related cases.
     SECTION 2.  The sum appropriated in section 1 shall be expended by the judiciary for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
