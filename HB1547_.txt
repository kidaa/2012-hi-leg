STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWENTY-THIRD REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     1.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Ala Wai small boat harbor, Oahu
          Construction of new fixed aluminum framed finger piers for 700 row pier replacement, utilities, and appurtenances.
          Construction                          $1,300,000
          Total funding                         $1,300,000
     B.   Piers, statewide
          Construction for repair or reconstruction of badly deteriorated or damaged piers.
          Construction                          $1,100,000
          Total funding                         $1,100,000      C.   Ala Wai small boat harbor, Oahu
          Construction for replacement of 500 row floating docks.
          Construction                          $1,900,000
          Total funding                         $1,900,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
