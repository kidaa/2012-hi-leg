STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE ELEVENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $30,195,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2011-2012 for the purpose of financing the capital improvement projects listed in this Act.
     I.   To be expended by the Department of Education:
          A.   Kihei Elementary School, Maui
(2)  Portable classroom for special education students.
                   Construction/materials            $375,000
                   Total Funding                     $375,000
          B.   Kihei High School, Maui
(1)  Land acquisition for new high school.
                   Construction                    $14,735,000
                   Total Funding                   $14,735,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement project authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
