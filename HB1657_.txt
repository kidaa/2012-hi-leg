A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE THIRTEENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The thirteenth representative district encompasses the area of East Maui, including Paia, Kuau, Haiku, Honopou, Kailua, Huelo, Keanae, Wailua, Hana, Kipahulu, and Kaupo and the islands of Lanai, Kahoolawe, Molokini, and Molokai, including Kalaupapa.  This is the only and last "canoe" district or disconnected legislative district.  Thus, each island area has vastly different capital improvement project needs.
     The purpose of this Act is to appropriate funds for capital improvement projects for the thirteenth representative district.
