TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FOURTEENTH, FIFTEENTH, AND SIXTEENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICTS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012:
     1.   To be expended by the county of Kauai:
     A.   Wailua-Kapaa water systems, Kauai
          Construction for Kapaa storage tank fire protection            improvements.
          Construction                              $3,000,000
          Total funding                             $3,000,000
     2.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Kauai high school, Kauai
          Plans, design, and construction for a new gymnatorium.
          Plans                                         $1,000
          Design                                      $100,000
          Construction                             $10,835,000
          Total funding                            $10,936,000
     3.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Wainiha bridge replacement project, Kauai
          Construction for the bridge replacement project.
          Construction                             $40,000,000
          Total funding                            $40,000,000
     4.   To be expended by the department of agriculture:
     A.   East Kauai irrigation system, east Kauai water users'               cooperative, Kauai
          Operation and maintenance of the east Kauai irrigation         system.
          Operation and Maintenance                   $100,000
          Total funding                               $100,000
     5.  To be expended by the department of land and natural resources:
     A.   Roadway reconstruction, mile marker 18 to Kalalau lookout, Kauai
          Design and reconstruction to renovate and resurface                roadway width varies from eighteen feet wide to twenty    feet wide, including drainage improvements.
          Design                                       $50,000
          Construction                              $7,950,000
          Total funding                             $8,000,000      B.   Polihale state park, Kauai
          Plans and design for a study for site maintenance              program for existing five-mile long access road to                 Polihale state park and possible realignment of                    existing access road over lands encumbered by the                  Agribusiness Development Corporation.
          Plans                                        $80,000
          Design                                      $120,000
          Total funding                               $200,000
     6.  To be expended by the department of accounting and general services:
     A.   Kapaa library, Kauai
          Design for a site feasibility study.
          Design                                      $250,000
          Total funding                               $250,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
