HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
1560
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO KAHANA VALLEY.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The department of land and natural resources is authorized to subdivide and provide for the creation of a residential subdivision in Kahana Valley, identified as tax map key parcels (1) 5-2-01:1, (1) 5-2-02, and (1) 5-2-05:1 and :21, situate at Kahana Valley, for use of the State or the department of land and natural resources, which shall be exempt from all statutes, ordinances, charter provisions, and rules of any governmental agency relating to zoning and construction standards for subdivisions, the development and improvement of land, and the construction of units thereon; provided that the project meets minimum requirements of health and safety.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
