A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE THIRTY-SEVENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

SECTION 1.  Mililani and Waipio Gentry's thirty-seventh representative district encompasses the new and expanding area of Waipio Gentry and older areas of Mililani.  The aging schools serving the residents of the district face deteriorating facilities in need of repair and maintenance to ensure that they can continue to meet our youth's educational needs.
