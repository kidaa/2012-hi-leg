STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FORTY-FIFTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2012-2013:
     1.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Waianae high school, Oahu
Design and construction for the merging of SP and T buildings to create additional classrooms and a production area.
Design                                  $300,000
Construction                          $2,700,000
Total funding                         $3,000,000
     B.   Waianae high school, Oahu
Design and construction for rubberized all-weather track and field.
Design                                  $150,000
Construction                          $1,350,000
Total funding                         $1,500,000
     C.   Waianae intermediate school, Oahu
Design and construction for the installation of security cameras.
Design                                    $5,000
Construction                             $45,000
Total funding                            $50,000
     D.   Kamaile elementary school, Oahu
Plans, land, and design for school expansion.
Plans                                    $50,000
Land                                    $250,000
Design                                  $200,000
Total funding                           $500,000
     E.   Leihoku elementary school, Oahu
Design and construction for the installation of security cameras.
Design                                    $1,000
Construction                              $9,000
Total funding                            $10,000
     F.   Makaha elementary school, Oahu
Construction for Americans with Disabilities Act transition accessibility.
Construction                            $375,000
Total funding                           $375,000
     G.   Waianae elementary school, Oahu
Design and construction of a new school administration building.
Design                                $1,000,000
Construction                          $4,000,000
Total funding                         $5,000,000
     2.   To be expended by the department of land and natural resources:
A.   Waianae small boat harbor, Oahu
     Construction for road and parking repairs.
     Construction                          $1,500,000
     Total funding                         $1,500,000
B.   Makua Kaena state park, Oahu
Design and construction for a pump facility and service line repair.
Design                                   $50,000
Construction                            $250,000
Total funding                           $300,000
C.   Puhawai Culvert, Oahu
Construction for Puhawai culvert replacement.
Construction                          $2,000,000
Total funding                         $2,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agencies for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
