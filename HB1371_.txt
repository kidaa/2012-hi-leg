TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE SIXTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $52,500,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2011-2012 for the following capital improvement projects for the sixth representative district:
1.   To be expended by the judiciary:
     A.   Kona courthouse, Hawaii
          Next phase of the building process.
          Land acquisition                          $ 5,000,000
          Design, civil engineering, site grading  $ 6,000,000
               Total funding                        $11,000,000
2.   To be expended by the Hawaii health systems corporation:
     A.   Kona hospital, Hawaii
          Repair and renovation of fire sprinkler system, emergency power, heating and ventilation system, facility master plan, roofing, and emergency room expansion.
               Total funding                        $13,000,000
3.   To be expended by the department of accounting and general services:
     A.   West Hawaii community health center, Hawaii
          Expansion of the center, including a drug treatment room, pediatric dental, and satellite clinic.
               Total funding                        $ 1,000,000
4.   To be expended by the University of Hawaii:
     A.   West Hawaii community college, Hawaii
          Expansion of second campus building complex
          Construction                              $15,000,000
               Total funding                        $15,000,000
5.   To be expended by the department of business, economic development, and tourism:
     A.   Natural energy laboratory of Hawaii authority, Hawaii
          Design and construction upgrades to seawater distribution system to support installation of a one-megawatt ocean thermal to energy conversion plant.
          Upgrade                                   $ 5,000,000
               Total funding                        $ 5,000,000
     B.   Natural energy laboratory of Hawaii authority, Hawaii
          Repairs to the forty inch seawater pipeline.
          Upgrade and repairs                       $ 3,500,000
               Total funding                        $ 3,500,000
     C.   Natural energy laboratory of Hawaii authority, Hawaii
          Comprehensive environmental impact statement
               Total funding                        $ 2,000,000
6.   To be expended by the department of Hawaiian home lands:
     A.   Laiopua 2020 community center, Hawaii
          Site civil engineering work
               Total funding                        $   500,000
7.   To be expended by the charter school administrative office of the department of education:
     A.   West Hawaii exploration academy, Hawaii
          Existing and new facility will be on state property located on land under management of the natural energy laboratory of Hawaii authority.
          Design and plans                         $ 1,500,000
               Total funding                        $ 1,500,000
     SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 3.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agencies for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
