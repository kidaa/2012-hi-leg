HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION
 
 
RELATING TO RECESS DAYS FOR THE REGULAR SESSION OF 2012.
 
 
 

     BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Twenty-sixth Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2012, the Senate concurring, that pursuant to Article III, section 10, of the Hawaii State Constitution, the dates of the mandatory recess for the Regular Session of 2012 are hereby determined to be the 23rd, 24th, 27th, 28th, and 29th days of February, on which dates the Regular Session of 2012 shall be recessed; and
 
     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that pursuant to Article III, section 10, of the Hawaii State Constitution, the dates of the recesses other than the dates of the mandatory recess, for the Regular Session of 2012 are hereby determined to be the 24th and 26th days of January, the 5th, 7th, and 29th days of March, the 9th, 11th, and 30th days of April, and the 2nd day of May, on which dates the Regular Session of 2012 shall be recessed.
