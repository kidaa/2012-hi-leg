     E.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          Air conditioning
          Total funding                         $3,500,000
     F.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          Electrical upgrade
          Total funding                           $400,000
     G.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          Drainage improvements
          Total funding                           $200,000
     H.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          ADA improvements buildings F and I
          Total funding                           $100,000
     I.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          Pedestrian bridge accessibility
          Total funding                           $300,000
     J.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          ADA improvements building I

     K.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
