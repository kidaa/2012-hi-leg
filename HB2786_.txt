Report Title:
General Appropriations Act of 2011; Ewa Elementary School
 
Description:
Amends the General Appropriations Act of 2011 to re-allocate funding for Ewa Elementary School to include design, construction, and equipment for a teachers' lounge and fourteen-classroom building.  Appropriation.  Effective July 1, 2012.
