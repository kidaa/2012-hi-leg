A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS MADE OUT OF THE EMERGENCY AND BUDGET RESERVE FUND PURSUANT TO ACT 191, SESSION LAWS OF HAWAII 2010.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Pursuant to Act 191, Session Laws of Hawaii 2010, the legislature appropriated moneys from the emergency and budget reserve fund to maintain levels of essential programs for education, human services, health, and the public welfare.  As of December 2010, the new governor is in the process of releasing the funds appropriated pursuant to Act 191.  However, due to the delay in the release of funds the expending agencies may not have enough time to expend the funds before they lapse at the end of the 2010-2011 fiscal year, thereby severely curtailing their ability to help the persons recognized by the legislature as being in dire need of these essential services.
     The purpose of this Act is to extend to December 31, 2011 the date on which the funds will lapse to ensure the fulfillment of the legislature's intent in passing Act 191, Session Laws of Hawaii 2010.

Report Title:
Emergency and Budget Reserve Fund; Appropriation
 
Description:
Extends date of lapse for funds appropriated pursuant to Act 191, Session Laws of Hawaii 2010 to December 31, 2011.  Appropriates moneys for fiscal year 2010-2011 for public health, safety, and welfare.  Extends date of lapse of funds appropriated for that purpose to December 31, 2011.
