TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWENTY-THIRD REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2012-2013 to be expended by the department of business, economic development, and tourism:
     A.   Waikiki community center, Oahu
          Plans, design, construction, and equipment for improvements to make the center comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act.     
          Plans                                    $35,200
          Design                                   $54,200
          Construction                            $217,040
          Equipment                               $367,560
          Total funding                           $674,000
     B.   Waikiki community center, Oahu
          Plans, design, construction, and equipment to replace roofing, gutters, and exterior building painting.
          Plans                                    $10,000
          Design                                    $5,000
          Construction                             $76,000
          Equipment                               $155,000
          Total funding                           $246,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.

Report Title:
CIP; 23rd Representative District
 
Description:
Appropriates funds for capital improvement projects for the Waikiki Community Center in the 23rd representative district.  Effective July 1, 2012.
