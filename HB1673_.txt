HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
1673
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO PUBLIC LIBRARIES.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 312, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows:
     "§312-     Brian T. Taniguchi Public Library.  The public library operated and maintained in Manoa, city and county of Honolulu, formerly under the name of Manoa Public Library shall be designated and known as the "Brian T. Taniguchi Public Library"."
     SECTION 2.  All references to "Manoa Public Library" in the records and public documents of the state library system, the department of education, the department of accounting and general services, the department of budget and finance, and any other relevant state department shall be replaced with "Brian T. Taniguchi Public Library".
     SECTION 3.  New statutory material is underscored.      SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
