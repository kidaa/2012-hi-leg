STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF THE ATTORNEY GENERAL.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  In November 2011, Honolulu will host the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Leaders Meeting.  APEC is the premier forum for Asia-Pacific economies to discuss trade and investment issues.  The President of the United States, heads of state, cabinet ministers, business leaders, government officials, and many others are expected to attend the APEC meeting.
     It is imperative that the State of Hawaii provide a safe venue for the APEC meeting.  The funding requested in this Act will support the department of the attorney general's efforts in planning for the event and provide funding for law enforcement personnel and their related operating and equipment costs, and computers and software, to ensure a successful and safe APEC meeting.
     SECTION 2.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $ _________, or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2011-2012, to carry out the purposes of this Act, including operating expenses relating to the planning and operation of security services and activities for the 2011 Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Leaders Meeting in Honolulu, Hawaii.
     The sum appropriated shall be expended by the department of the attorney general for the purposes of this Act.
SECTION 3.  The Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.

Report Title:
Appropriations; Department of the Attorney General; Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Leaders Meeting
 
Description:
Appropriates funds to the Department of the Attorney General for operating expenses of state law enforcement agencies relating to the planning and operation of security services and activities for the 2011 Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Leaders Meeting in Honolulu, Hawaii.
