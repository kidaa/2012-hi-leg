HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.C.R. NO.

TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
HOUSE CONCURRENT
RESOLUTION
 
 
REQUESTING THE GOVERNOR TO ADDRESS THE LEGISLATURE ASSEMBLED IN JOINT SESSION.
 
 
 

     BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Twenty-sixth Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2012, the Senate concurring, that the two Houses of the Legislature assemble in Joint Session in the Chambers of the House of Representatives at 10:00 a.m. on Monday, January 23, 2012, for the purpose of receiving such address or greetings from the Governor of Hawaii as he should be pleased to make; and
 
     BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a duly certified copy of this Concurrent Resolution be transmitted to the Honorable Neil Abercrombie, Governor of the State of Hawaii.
