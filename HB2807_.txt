TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF HAWAIIAN HOME LANDS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The auditor shall conduct a comprehensive financial audit of the department of Hawaiian home lands for fiscal year 2011-2012.  The audit shall focus on, but not be limited to an assessment of grants provided and contracts entered onto and administered by the department of Hawaiian home lands. 
     SECTION 2.  The auditor shall submit a report with findings and recommendations, including any proposed legislation, to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the regular session of 2013.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
