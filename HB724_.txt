STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO FOSTER CARE SERVICES.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The department of human services shall amend its rules under section 17-1617-13, Hawaii Administrative Rules, to increase from $529 per month to $      per month the monthly foster care maintenance payment to licensed foster family boarding homes, child-caring institutions, and facilities that provide contracted emergency shelter or group home services for the department of human services, office of youth services, or family court.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.

Report Title:
Foster Care; Monthly Maintenance Payments
 
Description:
Requires the Department of Human Services to amend its rules to increase the monthly foster care maintenance payment from $529 per month to an unspecified amount per month.  Effective on July 1, 2011.
