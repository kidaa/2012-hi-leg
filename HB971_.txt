HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
971
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  (a)  By July 1, 2012, the department of education shall increase fees for services provided by the office of school facilities and support services to cover not less than one-half of the cost of those services; provided that the school meals program shall be exempt from the fee increase.
     (b)  This Act shall not apply where the applicability would jeopardize the receipt of any federal funds.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Department of Education; Fees
 
Description:
Requires DOE to increase fees for services provided by the office of school facilities and support services to cover at least 50% of the cost of those services.  Exempts the school meals program from the increase in fees.
