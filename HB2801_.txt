TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE SEVENTEENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $31,555,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2012-2013 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the benefit of the seventeenth representative district, as follows:
     A.   Hahaione elementary school, Oahu
(1)  Planning, design and construction to upgrade electrical system.
              Planning                                $25,000
              Design                                  $40,000
              Construction                          $400,000
              Total Funding                         $465,000
(2)  Planning, design and construction to air condition school.
              Planning                              $250,000
              Design                                $250,000
              Construction                          $3,000,000
               Total Funding                         $3,500,000
(3)  Planning, design and construction to renovate room H-2, building H.
              Planning                                $50,000
              Design                                  $50,000
              Construction                          $350,000
              Total Funding                         $450,000
     B.   Kaiser high school, Oahu
(1)  Planning, design and construction of a performing arts auditorium.
              Planning                              $500,000
              Design                                $500,000
              Construction                         $16,000,000
              Total Funding                        $17,000,000
(2)  Planning, design and construction to replace football field grass with synthetic turf.
              Planning                              $100,000
              Design                                $100,000
              Construction                          $2,000,000
              Total Funding                         $2,200,000
(3)  Planning, design and construction to replace track.
              Planning                              $100,000
              Design                                $100,000
              Construction                          $2,000,000
              Total Funding                         $2,200,000
(4)  Planning, design and construction to upgrade electrical system.
              Planning                              $100,000
              Design                                $100,000
              Construction                          $1,200,000
              Total Funding                        $1,400,000
     C.   Kamiloiki elementary school, Oahu
(1)  Planning, design and construction to upgrade electrical system.
              Planning                                $25,000
              Design                                  $40,000
              Construction                          $400,000
              Total Funding                         $465,000
(2)  Planning, design and construction to air condition school.
              Planning                              $250,000
              Design                                $250,000
              Construction                          $3,000,000
              Total Funding                         $3,500,000
(3)  Planning, design and construction for ADA transition accessibility.
              Planning                                $25,000
              Design                                  $50,000
              Construction                          $300,000
              Total Funding                         $375,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated shall be expended by the department of education for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  The appropriations made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriations unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
