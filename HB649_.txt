STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWENTY-THIRD REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for the fiscal year 2011-2012:
     1.   To be expended by the department of land and natural           resources:
     A.   Ala Wai small boat harbor, Oahu
          Construction for improvements to B, C, and D floating              decks.
          Construction                          $3,000,000
          Total funding                         $3,000,000
     B.   Ala Wai small boat harbor, Oahu
          Construction for improvement to 700 rows.
          Construction                          $1,000,000
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     C.   Kewalo Basin, Oahu                                             Construction for miscellaneous bike path improvements.
          Construction                          $1,910,000
          Total funding                         $1,910,000
     D.   Waikiki sea wall, Oahu                              
          Construction for improvements to the Waikiki sea wall.
          Construction                          $1,910,000
          Total funding                         $1,910,000
     2.   To be expended by the department education:
     A.   Jefferson elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for restroom renovations for building O.
          Construction                            $100,000
          Total funding                           $100,000
     B.   Jefferson elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for electrical system upgrades for
          building R.
          Construction                            $100,000
          Total funding                           $100,000
     C.   Ala Wai elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for campus drainage improvements.
          Construction                            $150,000
          Total funding                           $150,000
     D.   Ala Wai elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for campus carpentry work.
          Construction                             $35,000
          Total funding                            $35,000
     E.   Waikiki elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for installation of the central public            address system.
          Construction                              $5,000
          Total funding                             $5,000
     F.   Waikiki elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for installation of fence for                     kindergarten area.
          Construction                              $9,000
          Total funding                             $9,000
     G.   Waikiki elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for installation of security lights.
          Construction                              $3,000
          Total funding                             $3,000
     H.   Waikiki Community Center, Oahu
          Construction for installation of safety padding for            preschool playground.
          Construction                            $150.000
          Total funding                           $150,000
 
     I.   Waikiki Community Center, Oahu
          Construction costs for roof repair, gutter replacement         and installation, and painting of three buildings in          the complex that houses five nonprofit agencies,                            including Waikiki Health Center's main health clinic.         Project      qualifies as a grant pursuant to chapter 42F,                 Hawaii Revised Statues.
          Construction                            $230,000
          Total funding                           $230,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
