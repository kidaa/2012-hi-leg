TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO THE HAWAII ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT TASK FORCE.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Section 6 of Act 73, Session Laws of Hawaii 2010, is amended by amending subsection (g) to read as follows:
     "(g)  The Hawaii economic development task force shall cease to exist on June 30, [2012.] 2014."
     SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on June 29, 2012.
 
INTRODUCED BY:
_____________________________
 
 
 

 
Report Title:
Economic Development Task Force
 
Description:
Extends the sunset date for the Hawaii economic development task force to 6/30/14.  Effective 6/29/12.
