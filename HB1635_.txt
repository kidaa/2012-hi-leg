HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO OUT-OF-STATE MARRIAGES.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Section 572-3, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended to read as follows:
     "§572-3 Contracted without the State.  Marriages between a man and a woman legal in the country where contracted shall be held legal in the courts of this State.  Marriages which are legal in the United States jurisdiction where contracted shall be held legal in the courts of this state."
     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
 
INTRODUCED BY:
_____________________________
 
 
 

 
Report Title:
Out-of-State Marriages
 
Description:
Requires Hawaii state courts to recognize marriages legal in the United States' jurisdiction in which they were contracted.
