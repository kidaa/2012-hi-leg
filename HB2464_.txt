STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO LANDOWNERS' LIABILITY.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The purpose of this Act is to amend section 520-2, Hawaii Revised Statutes, to include lands owned by the government.
     SECTION 2.  Section 520-2, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by amending the definition of "land" to read as follows:
     ""Land" means land, roads, water, watercourses, private ways and buildings, structures, and machinery or equipment when attached to realty[, other than lands owned by the government]."
     SECTION 3.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
