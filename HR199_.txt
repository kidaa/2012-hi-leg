HOUSE RESOLUTION
 
 
AUTHORIZING AND EMPOWERING THE SPEAKER TO PERFORM AND CARRY OUT ANY OFFICIAL LEGISLATIVE BUSINESS DURING THE INTERIM BETWEEN THE 2012 AND 2013 REGULAR SESSIONS.
 
 
 

BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Twenty-sixth Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2012, that the Speaker be, and is hereby authorized and empowered during the interim between the 2012 and 2013 Regular Sessions, to:
 
     (1)  Expend such sum or sums from the funds appropriated for legislative and other expenses of the Twenty-sixth Legislature, Regular Session of 2012;
 
     (2)  Contract, hire, or employ personnel and assistance, including:
 
(A)  Designating which of the employees and officers of the House shall be given additional employment to meet the after-session work demands; and
 
(B)  Approving the employment of individuals prior to the convening of the Regular Session of 2013;
 
and the period of employment for each;
 
     (3)  Establish and approve any action of standing, interim, and special committees, including:
 
(A)  Authorizing meetings to obtain information and conduct hearings;

(B)  Determining members, functions, and duties of such committees; provided that the Speaker, notwithstanding any prior authorization or action, may, at the Speaker's discretion, limit the number of members of the House participating on the committee;
 
(C)  Issuing subpoenas as provided by law or rules of the House;
 
(D)  Authorizing in writing the payment of any committee related expenditure prior to the payment being made or incurred; and
 
(E)  Implementing a requirement that a full report of all activities of the committees, including their respective findings and recommendations, shall be made periodically to the Speaker as the Speaker so desires, and to the Legislature within ten days after the convening of the Regular Session of 2013; and
 
     (4)  Approve the Journal of the House of any legislative day being compiled as of the 60th session day,
 
all for the purposes of completing the work of the Twenty-sixth Legislature, Regular Session of 2012, subsequent to the adjournment thereof, including the carrying out of any official legislative business in the interim between the 2012 and 2013 sessions, up to and including January 16, 2013, this authorization being in addition to any other authorization heretofore granted to the Speaker or any other officer or officers of the House of Representatives.
