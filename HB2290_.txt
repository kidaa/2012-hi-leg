STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CHILDREN AND YOUTH.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Section 8-11, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended to read as follows:
     "§8‑11  Children and Youth Day and Month.  The first Sunday in October shall be known and designated as "Children and Youth Day", and the entire month of October shall be known and designated as "Children and Youth Month".  Public events celebrating Children and Youth Day may be held on the grounds of the state capitol on the first Sunday in October, unless otherwise determined by the governor.  This day and month are not and shall not be construed to be state holidays."
     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Children and Youth Day and Month; Public Events; Capitol Grounds
 
Description:
Authorizes public events celebrating Children and Youth Day to be held on state capitol grounds on the first Sunday in October, unless otherwise determined by the governor.
