STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO ELECTED PUBLIC OFFICERS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 11, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows:
     "§11-    Mandatory service of entire elected term.  Any elected public officer of the State or county shall serve the entire term of the office for which the person was elected, subject to the person's death or debilitating physical or psychological incapacity."
     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Elected Public Officers; Mandatory Terms
 
Description:
Mandates that elected public officers serve the entire term of office for which they are elected, subject to certain conditions.
