TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO EDUCATION.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 302A, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows:
     "§302A-     Indemnity upon intervening in crime-related incidents; students.  The State shall indemnify and hold harmless any department employee intervening in good faith to prevent any act:
     (1)  By a student enrolled in a public school in the State;
     (2)  Occurring on or off of school property;
     (3)  During or after school hours; and
     (4)  Involving criminal activity;
provided that indemnification shall be from any civil liability that might otherwise be incurred or imposed by, or as a result of, the intervention."
     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Education
 
Description:
Indemnifies department of education employees who intervene to prevent criminal activities of students on or off campus during or after school hours.
