HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2368
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII AT HILO.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $          or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2012-2013 for the establishment of a medical residency program at the University of Hawaii at Hilo under program ID UOH 210.
     The sum appropriated shall be expended by the University of Hawaii at Hilo for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
