STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FOURTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     1.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Pahoa high school, Hawaii
          Construction and equipment for a gymnasium.
          Construction                              $1,500,000
          Equipment                                 $1,500,000
          Total funding                             $3,000,000
     B.   Pahoa elementary school, Hawaii
          Plans, design, and construction for a new cafeteria.
          Plans                                       $450,000
          Design                                      $450,000
          Construction                              $8,100,000
          Total funding                             $9,000,000      C.   Pahoa elementary school, Hawaii
          Equipment for electrical system upgrade.
          Equipment                                   $909,000
          Total funding                               $909,000
     D.   Keonepoko elementary school, Hawaii
          Design and construction for covered walkways to                portable buildings P10 and P11.
          Design                                      $100,000
          Construction                                $100,000
          Total funding                               $200,000
     E.   Keonepoko elementary school, Hawaii
          Equipment for electrical system upgrade.
          Equipment                                   $435,000
          Total funding                               $435,000
     F.   Keonepoko elementary school, Hawaii
          Design and construction for the extension of the               parking lot.
          Design                                      $200,000
          Construction                                $200,000
          Total Funding                               $400,000      G.   Kua o Ka La public charter school, Hawaii
          Construction and equipment for the repair and                  maintenance of two classrooms.
          Construction                                $250,000
          Equipment                                   $250,000
          Total funding                               $500,000
     2.   To be expended by the department of Hawaiian home lands:
     A.   Makuu farmers association, Hawaii
          Plans and design for a community center for the Makuu              farmers association.
          Plans                                       $250,000
          Design                                      $250,000
          Total funding                               $500,000
     3.   To be expended by the department of transportation
     A.   Puna Makai alternate route, Hawaii
          Design the Puna Makai alternate route to connect               Nanawale estates subdivision with the city of Hilo to         improve vehicular, pedestrian, bicycle, and                                     transit circulation and safety.
          Design                                      $500,000
          Total funding                               $500,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.  SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
