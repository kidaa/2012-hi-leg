HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
384
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE DEPARTMENT OF WATER COUNTY OF KAUAI.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     To be expended by the county of Kauai:
     A.   Kilauea water system, Kauai
     Kilauea school waterline fire protection improvements.
               Construction                         $1,000,000
                   Total funding                    $1,000,000
     B.   Wailua-Kapaa water systems, Kauai
     Kapaa storage tank fire protection improvements.
              Construction                         $3,000,000
                   Total funding                    $3,000,000
     C.   Hanalei water system, Kauai
     Hanalei school waterline fire protection improvements.
              Construction                         $3,000,000
                   Total funding                    $3,000,000
     D.   Wainiha-Haena water system, Kauai
     Design and construction of a 100,000-gallon storage tank, booster pump station, connecting pipeline, and appurtenances for fire protection improvements.
              Construction                         $3,000,000
                   Total funding                    $3,000,000
     E.   Wailua-Kapaa water system, Kauai
     Kawaihau road section, Kahuna and Piliamoo roads 12-inch and 16-inch water mains.  Water main design and construction for affordable housing infrastructure development.
              Construction                         $4,500,000
                   Total funding                    $4,500,000
     F.   Wailua-Kapaa water system, Kauai
     Drill, test, and develop Kapaa homesteads well number four for affordable housing infrastructure development.
              Construction                       $    600,000
                   Total funding                  $    600,000
     G.   Kekaha water system, Kauai
     Kekaha (Amfac) shaft and 12-inch waterline.  Design and construction of a new water source and connecting pipeline for affordable housing infrastructure development.
              Construction                         $3,000,000
                   Total funding                    $3,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
