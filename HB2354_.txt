STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO VETERANS.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sum, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the project listed in this Act, is hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2012-2013:
     1.  To be expended by the department of defense:
A.  Veterans care home, statewide
        Land acquisition, plans, design, and construction
        for a veterans care home.
        Land acquisition                           $1
        Plans                                  $1,000
        Design                                           $1
        Construction                              $1
        Total funding                         $1,003
     SECTION 2.  The sum appropriated for the respective capital project set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.      SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.

Report Title:
Veterans Care Home; Capital Improvement; Appropriation

Provides funds for the acquisition of land, planning, design, and construction of a care home facility for veterans.  Effective July 1, 2012.
