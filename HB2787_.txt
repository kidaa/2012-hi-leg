TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FORTY-SECOND REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $4,272,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary, and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2012-2013 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the forty-second representative district, as follows:
1.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   James Campbell high school, Oahu
          Design and construction for electrical upgrades.
          Design                                     $   85,000
          Construction                                  900,000
              Total funding                         $  985,000
     B.   Waipahu high school, Oahu,
          Plans, design, and construction to complete fencing project.
          Plans                                      $    1,000
          Design                                          1,000
          Construction                                   73,000
              Total funding                         $   75,000
     C.   Waipahu intermediate school, Oahu Design and construction for a fence along the back of campus.
          Design                                     $   10,000
          Construction                                   90,000
              Total funding                         $  100,000
2.   To be expended by the state civil defense:
     A.   Honouliuli flood mitigation, Oahu Study, planning, and design, or as matching funds for federal emergency management agency appropriations, for Honouliuli flood mitigation project in Ewa.
          Study, planning, and design         $  100,000
              Total funding                         $  100,000
3.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Renton road and Roosevelt avenue, Oahu
          Plans, design, and construction to add a street light at the intersection.
          Plans                                      $    1,000
          Design                                          1,000
          Construction                                   10,000
               Total funding                         $   12,000
     B.   Kunia road, Oahu
          Plans, design, and construction to extend third lane over Farrington highway to the H-1 Freeway southbound to the vicinity of Laulaunui street.
          Plans, design, and construction     $3,000,000
              Total funding                         $3,000,000
     SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation that are unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 3.  The sum appropriated for the respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
