HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
834
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO FIREARMS AND AMMUNITION.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  Chapter 134, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately designated and to read as follows:
     "§134-     Restriction on sale of ammunition.  It shall be unlawful for any person, including a licensed manufacturer, licensed importer, or licensed dealer, to sell or distribute any ammunition to a person unless the person provides proof that the firearm for which the ammunition is to be used has been registered pursuant to this part; provided that a licensed dealer may sell ammunition for a firearm at the time the firearm is sold."
     SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
 
Firearms; Ammunition Sales
 
Description:
Makes it unlawful for a person or entity to sell or distribute ammunition unless the purchaser provides proof that the firearm for which the ammunition is to be used is registered.
