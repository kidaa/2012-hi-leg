Capital Improvement Projects; Thirty-fifth, Thirty-seventh, Fortieth, Forty-first, and Forty-second Representative District Schools

Appropriates funds for capital improvement projects for schools in the thirty-fifth, thirty-seventh, fortieth, forty-first, and forty-second representative districts.  Effective July 1, 2012.
