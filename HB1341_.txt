A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE TWELFTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     1.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Old Haleakala sidewalks, Maui
Plans, design, and construction of sidewalk along one
side of old Haleakala highway from Kula highway to Pukalani street.  Partial matching funds to be provided by the County of Maui.
Plans                                   $ 50,000
Design                                  $250,000
Construction                            $700,000
          Total funding                         $1,000,000
     B.   Haleakala highway improvements, Maui
          Construction to install right turn lane on west-bound
          Haleakala highway, install walkways and paved shoulder and/or sidewalks from intersection to King Kekaulike high school entrances, and to make traffic signal improvements, signage, markings, and other related improvements.
          Construction                          $1,500,000
          Total funding                         $1,500,000

     A.   Upcountry Maui, Maui

          Total funding                         $2,500,000

          Phase 2 design and construction      $13,000,000
          Total funding                        $38,000,000
     C.   Kula elementary school, Maui
          Design, construction, and equipment for expansion of the cafeteria.                                     
Design                                  $250,000
Construction                          $6,500,000
Equipment                               $250,000

Design                                  $125,000
Construction                          $3,800,000 Equipment                                $75,000
