TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE THIRTY-FIFTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012:
     1.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Waipahu high school, Oahu
          Design and construction for electrical upgrades.
          Design                                       $500,000
          Construction                              $4,000,000
          Total funding                             $4,500,000
     B.   August Ahrens elementary school, Oahu
          Design and construction for electrical upgrades.
          Design                                       $300,000
          Construction                              $1,500,000
          Total funding                             $1,800,000      C.   August Ahrens elementary school, Oahu
          Design and construction for a new classroom building.
          Design                                      $800,000
          Construction                              $9,200,000
          Total funding                            $10,000,000
     D.   Waipahu elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for a new classroom building.
          Construction                              $8,100,000
          Total funding                             $8,100,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
