HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.
2879
TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2012
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE THIRTEENTH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $39,923,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary, and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2012-2013 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the thirteenth representative district, as follows:
1.   To be expended by the department of accounting and general services:
     Huelo community center, Maui
          Planning, design, and construction of a Huelo community center; provided that the county of Maui match the funds appropriated by the State
              Total funding                           $250,000
2.   To be expended by the department of defense:
     Molokai veteran's center
          For completion of new veterans resources building and health center; provided that the office of Hawaiian affairs will provide a partial match of $1 for every $2 appropriated by the State
               Total funding                           $250,000
3.   To be expended by the department of education:
     Lanai high and elementary school
          Phase II construction of six classroom buildings
              Total funding                         $8,000,000
4.   To be expended by the department of health:
     A.   Hana health center
          Renovation and expansion of the health center; provided that the office of Hawaiian affairs will provide a partial match of $1 for every $2 appropriated by the State
              Total funding                         $1,000,000
     B.   Lanai community health center
          Planning, design, and construction for a new community health center; to match federal funds in the planning; provided that the office of Hawaiian affairs will provide a partial match of $1 for every $2 appropriated by the State
              Total funding                         $1,000,000
     C.   Molokai community health center
          Renovations to the health center; provided that the office of Hawaiian affairs will provide a partial match of $1 for every $2 appropriated by the State
              Total funding                         $1,000,000
     D.   Lanai community hospital
          Construction, renovation, repair, and equipment replacement
              Total funding                         $9,098,000
5.   To be expended by the department of human services:
     A.   Nahiku community center, Maui
          center and parking lot improvements
              Total funding                           $250,000
     B.   Hana senior/physically challenged/low-income housing
          Planning, design, and construction of affordable housing
              Total funding                        $10,000,000
6.   To be expended by the department of agriculture:
     A.   Molokai irrigation system
          Pump additional water into irrigation system
              Total funding                           $500,000
     B.   Molokai irrigation system
          Continuance of repair and maintenance
              Total funding                         $2,500,000
7.   To be expended by the department of transportation:
     A.   Hana highway
          To place a name sign on each bridge on Hana highway to identify each bridge
              Total funding                            $75,000
     B.   Hana high school sidewalks
          Construction of sidewalks from the Hana high school entrance along the Hana highway
              Total funding                         $2,000,000
8.   To be expended by the University of Hawaii
     A.   Planning, design, and construction of the expansion for the Maui community college, Molokai campus
              Total funding                         $2,500,000
     B.   Planning, design, and construction of a building on Hawaiian home lands, for the hookulaiwi education program
               Total funding                         $1,500,000
     SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal year for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation that are unencumbered as of June 30, 2014, shall lapse as of that date.
     SECTION 3.  The sum appropriated for the respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 of this Act shall be expended by the designated expending agencies for the purposes of this Act.
     SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2012.
