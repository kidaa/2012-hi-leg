HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
H.B. NO.

TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO APPROPRIATIONS TO THE DEPARTMENT OF THE PROSECUTING ATTORNEY OF THE CITY AND COUNTY OF HONOLULU.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2011-2012 for a grant-in-aid to the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for the career criminal prosecution unit, including the hiring of necessary staff.
     SECTION 2.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2011-2012 for a grant-in-aid to the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for prosecution in drug court, including the hiring of necessary staff.
     SECTION 3.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2011-2012 for a grant-in-aid to the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for prosecution in Hawaii's opportunity probation with enforcement, including the hiring of necessary staff.
     SECTION 4.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2011-2012 for a grant-in-aid to the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for the victim witness assistance program, including the hiring of necessary staff.
     SECTION 5.  The sums appropriated in sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 shall be expended by the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for the purposes of this Act.
SECTION 6.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.

Report Title:
Appropriations; Honolulu Prosecuting Attorney
 
Description:
Appropriates funds to the department of the prosecuting attorney of the city and county of Honolulu for career criminal prosecution unit, prosecution in drug court, prosecution in Hawaii's opportunity probation with enforcement, and victim witness assistance program.
