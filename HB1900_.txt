STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO TAXATION.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  (a)  The department of taxation shall develop a plan, including proposed legislation, to implement a flat income tax rate of        per cent of taxable income that shall be ready for implementation no later than the 2015 taxable year.
     (b)  The flat tax shall replace the income tax schedule in section 235-51, Hawaii Revised Statutes, and shall dedicate
     per cent of the income tax revenues to the State's medicaid program under the department of human services.  The flat tax rate shall not exceed three per cent of an individual's taxable income.
     (c)  The department of taxation may work with the department of budget and finance, the department of human services, the legislature, and other departments as necessary, to develop the plan and proposed legislation.
     (d)  The plan and proposed legislation may include:
     (1)  Repealing tax deductions, exemptions, and credits, as needed to implement the flat tax;
     (2)  Gradual implementation until the flat tax is fully implemented in 2015;
     (3)  Establishing a special fund for the receipt of income tax revenues for the State's medicaid program; and
     (4)  Establishing a task force to recommend the appropriate tax rate to generate income tax revenues comparable to income tax revenues generated within the past five taxable years.
     (e)  The department of taxation shall submit progress reports to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the 2013 and 2014 regular sessions, and shall submit proposed legislation to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the 2014 regular session to implement the flat tax by the taxable year beginning after December 31, 2014.
     SECTION 2.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Report Title:
Flat Income Tax; Medicaid
 
Description:
Requires the Department of Taxation to develop a plan and proposed legislation to implement a flat income tax no later than the 2015 taxable year.  Dedicates a percentage of the flat income tax to medicaid.
