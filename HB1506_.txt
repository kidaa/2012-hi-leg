TWENTY-SIXTH LEGISLATURE, 2011
 
STATE OF HAWAII
 
 
 
 
 
 
A BILL FOR AN ACT
 
 
RELATING TO CAPITAL IMPROVEMENT PROJECTS FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE FORTIETH REPRESENTATIVE DISTRICT.
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
 

     SECTION 1.  The following sums, or so much thereof as shall be sufficient to finance the projects listed in this Act, are hereby appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii for fiscal year 2011-2012.
     1.   To be expended by the department of education:
     A.   Kapolei high school, Oahu
          Design and construction for athletic complex,                  including a locker room and two classrooms.
          Design                                      $442,000
          Construction                              $3,991,000
          Total funding                             $4,433,000
     B.   Kapolei middle school, Oahu
          Construction for removal of carpet and installation of         tile flooring in the physical education classroom for              health and safety purposes.
          Construction                                 $15,000
          Total funding                                $15,000      C.   Barbers Point elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for the reroofing of Building H.
          Construction                                $226,000
          Total funding                               $226,000
     D.   Barbers point elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for the renovation of the restrooms for               Building E.
          Construction                                $188,000
          Total funding                               $188,000
     E.   Makakilo elementary school, Oahu
          Construction for installation of backflow preventer to         regulate water pressure for school campus.
          Construction                                 $81,000
          Total funding                                $81,000
     SECTION 2.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the designated expending agency for the purposes of this Act.  SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2011.
